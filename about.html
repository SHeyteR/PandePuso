    • The Pan De Puso

    Pan de Puso is a business that introduces a unique banana cake infused with Filipino flavors by combining banana and ube—two fruits commonly used in Filipino cuisine. The goal is to show that these ordinary fruits can be transformed into a flavorful and heartwarming dessert. The banana-ube cake reflects Filipino tradition and culture, offering customers a nostalgic and delicious experience. In addition to the cake, Pan de Puso offers complementary products like a Banana Caramel Milkshake and a Peach Banana Pie—an original twist inspired by Jollibee. The brand aims to create a unique and satisfying food experience that highlights the richness of Filipino flavors.

    Mission

    Pan de Pusos purpose is to make fresh, quality, and authentic cake using local sources while encouraging healthy eating and creating memorable experiences

    Vision

    The vision of Pan De Puso for the future is to create a dessert shop that is more than just a shop, but a go-to place where people feel comfort in their food while maintaining the quality and authenticity.
